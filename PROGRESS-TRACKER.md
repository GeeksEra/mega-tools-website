## DOMAIN RESEARCH SUMMARY

Top recommendations from GoDaddy research:
1. toolsai.com — Check availability (ideal: short + AI in name)
2. aitools365.ai — AED 183.65/yr (AI extension, trendy)
3. alltoolsai.co — AED 1.00 first year (very cheap start)
4. smarttoolsai.com — Check availability
5. toolify.io — Check availability (.io is respected in tech)

Decision criteria:
- .com preferred for trust + global reach
- .ai works well for AI-focused branding
- .io is acceptable (trusted in developer community)
- Avoid: .net, .org, .info (less trusted for tools)
- Must be: Short (under 15 chars), memorable, professional
