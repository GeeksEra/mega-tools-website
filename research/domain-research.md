## Research Date: March 2026 | Source: GoDaddy Live Search

---

## DOMAIN REQUIREMENTS

Good domain for a mega tools site should be:
- Short: Under 15 characters ideally
- Memorable: Easy to say and remember
- Brandable: Doesn't sound generic/spammy
- Relevant: Contains "tools" and/or "AI" for branding
- TLD: .com preferred, .io or .ai acceptable for tech
- Avoid: .net, .org, .info, .biz (less trusted)

## TOP DOMAIN RECOMMENDATION

### #1 Recommendation: aifretools.com
- **Price:** AED 1 first year (then ~AED 85/yr after)
- **Why:** Contains "AI" + "free" + "tools" — directly answers user search intent
- **Brand value:** "aifree tools" = AI-powered free tools — perfect positioning
- **SEO value:** Keywords in domain (minor benefit but still helps)
- **TLD:** .com — Most trusted globally
- **Memorability:** Short, clear, easy to say
- **Verdict:** BEST VALUE OPTION

### #2 Recommendation: smarttoolsai.co
- **Price:** AED 1 first year (then ~AED 189/yr after)
- **Why:** "Smart Tools AI" — premium feel, positions as intelligent tools
- **Brand value:** Strong brand — "smart" + "tools" + "ai"
- **TLD:** .co — Accepted and respected (used by Twitter's t.co, etc.)
- **Memorability:** Good — 3 strong keywords
- **Verdict:** BEST BRANDING OPTION

### #3 Recommendation: Custom .io domain
- Consider names like: toolverse.io, toolhub.io, toolify.io
- .io domains are trusted in developer/tech communities
- Check availability at GoDaddy
- Budget: Usually AED 100-200/yr for .io

## DOMAIN STRATEGY NOTES

### Why ".com" Still Wins
- 46% of all domains are .com
- Users automatically type .com when guessing URLs
- Higher trust from general public
- Better for brand recognition long-term

### When .io or .ai is Acceptable
- Tech-savvy audience (developers, AI users)
- If .com is too expensive or unavailable
- Modern startups often use .io (GitHub.io, etc.)
- .ai is specific to AI branding (good for our use case)

### Budget Recommendation
- Start: Register .com for AED 1-85/yr
- Later: Also register same name with .co and .ai to protect brand
- Always: Enable auto-renew to not lose domain
- Privacy protection: Enable (usually free with GoDaddy)

## DOMAIN NAMING ALTERNATIVES TO EXPLORE

Good name patterns for tools sites:
- [word]tools.com (e.g., smarttools.com, aitools.com)
- [word]toolbox.com (e.g., aitoolbox.com)
- [word]toolkit.com (e.g., freetoolkit.com)
- tools[word].com (e.g., toolsverse.com, toolshub.com)
- free[word]tools.com (e.g., freeaitools.com)
- [word]hub.com (e.g., toolhub.com, aihub.com)

Check these on GoDaddy:

## AFTER REGISTERING

Immediate steps after domain registration:
1. Point DNS to Vercel (for hosting)
2. Enable Cloudflare for CDN + DDoS protection
3. Set up SSL certificate (free via Vercel or Cloudflare)
4. Enable domain privacy protection
5. Set up email forwarding (hello@yourdomain.com → your Gmail)
6. Register same name on social media: Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn
7. Add to Google Search Console as your property
8. Register on Google My Business (optional but helpful)
